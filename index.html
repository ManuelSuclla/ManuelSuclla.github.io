About Me

I'm Manuel Suclla, and it's a pleasure to meet you.

Soon to be graduating with my Bachelors in Computer Science Engineering has definitely been an adventure...

It all started in Venezuela, being born to the two most amazing Peruvian parents. After a few years, we moved and spent the latter half of my life in Miami, FL. My family sacrificed so much to get me a better education, and I used this opportunity to the fullest. I continued on to acquire my Associates degree from the Miami Dade Honors program in Computer Engineering. And just how my parents left their comfort zone, I did as well. From warm tropical Florida, to the harsh winters at the University of Michigan.. and here I am today.
