About Me

My name is Manuel Suclla, and it's a pleasure to meet you.

Soon to be graduating with my bachelors in Computer Science Engineering from University of Michigan has definitely been an adventure...

It all started in Venezuela, being born to the two most amazing Peruvian parents (Peruvian Food == Best food ever). Deep into my childhood, we ended up moving and spending the latter half of my life in Miami, FL. My family sacrificed so much to get me a better education. I studied, grinded, and moved onward. I continued on to get my Associates degree from the Miami Dade Honors program in Computer Engineering. And just how my parents left their comfort zone, I did as well. I decided to completely change my environment. From warm tropical Florida, to the harsh winters of Michigan.. and here I am today.
